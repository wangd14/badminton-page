Badminton Page

By: Weiheng Oh, Jeremia Silaban, David Wang

Badminton is the fastest sport in the world. The badminton birdie, also known as a shuttlecock, often travels over 200 miles per hour in professional badminton competitions when players smash. The world record for the fastest badminton smash is 665 km/h, which was performed in a controlled environment. The world record for the fastest badminton smash in a tournament is 426 km/h.
